> This page is about [Tibetan Mandala Coloring Pages](https://freecoloringpages.github.io/). The sacred art of sand painting comes from the Tibetan Buddhist tradition (Tib: dul-tson-kyil-khor – mandala of coloured powders; 'mandala' means circle in Sanskrit). Tibetan Buddhism (7th century CE) is based on Indian Buddhism (5th century CE), and its main goals are to reach individual enlightenment, the liberation of all beings, and the development of unconditional compassion and insight wisdom. [Source: https://www.ancient.eu/](https://www.ancient.eu/)

* Locate where the images will be saved in
